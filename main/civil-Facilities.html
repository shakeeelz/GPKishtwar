Civil Engineering Facilities

The Civil Engineering Department at Government Polytechnic College Kishtwar is equipped with modern laboratories and cutting-edge equipment to provide hands-on learning experiences. The facilities include surveying labs, geotechnical labs, and materials testing labs, all designed to help students gain practical skills relevant to the industry.

The department also features dedicated workspaces for project development, enabling students to work on real-world applications and innovative solutions. Students have access to a variety of tools, testing equipment, and resources to enhance their learning experience.

In addition, the department regularly organizes workshops, industrial visits, and expert lectures to keep students updated on the latest trends in the field of civil engineering. These initiatives help bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical applications.
