Admission Procedure

Admissions to Govt. Polytechnic College are usually conducted in the month of May/June every year. The J&K Board of Technical Education (JKBOTE) announces the application process through their portal: www.jksbotelive.com.

Eligibility: Minimum qualification is 10th pass with at least 35% marks. Admissions are based on merit, category/reservations, and preference. Seats are allotted in two rounds, and any left-over seats after the rounds are filled at the institution level in August every year.

For Lateral Entry into 2nd Year (3rd Semester), students with 10+2 (Non-Medical) or a 2-Year ITI diploma after 10th are eligible.
