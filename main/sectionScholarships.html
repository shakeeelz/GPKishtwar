Scholarships

Scholarships provide crucial financial support to students, enabling them to continue their education without financial burdens. The Government of India, along with the Government of Jammu and Kashmir, offers a variety of scholarships to assist students from different sections of society.

At Government Polytechnic College Kishtwar, we facilitate students in applying for these scholarships online, ensuring they receive the support they are eligible for. Below is a list of available scholarships, along with the approximate financial assistance provided under each scheme:

  • Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC/OBC (JKUT Scheme): This scheme offers financial assistance ranging from ₹230 to ₹1,200 per month, depending on the course and category. Students can apply through the JKUT Services Portal.
  • Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for Minorities: Under this scheme, students from minority communities can receive financial assistance of ₹3,000 to ₹6,000 per year. Applications can be submitted via the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).
  • Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for Students with Disabilities: Scholarships range from ₹230 to ₹1,200 per month. Eligible students can apply through the NSP.
  • Post Matric Scholarship for BEEDI/CINE/IOMC/LSDM Wards: Financial assistance varies between ₹1,000 and ₹5,000 per year. Applications are accepted on the NSP.
  • Merit-cum-Means Scholarship for Professional & Technical Courses: Students pursuing technical courses can receive up to ₹20,000 annually, plus a maintenance allowance. Apply via the NSP.
  • National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship: Awarded based on a national-level scholarship test, students can receive ₹12,000 per year. Applications can be submitted through the NSP.
  • PRAGATI Scholarship for Girls (Diploma Polytechnics): This scheme offers up to ₹50,000 annually for girl students pursuing diploma courses. Apply via the NSP.
  • SAKSHAM Scholarship for Specially Abled Students (Diploma Polytechnics): Specially abled students can receive up to ₹50,000 annually under this scheme. Applications can be submitted on the NSP.
  • Scholarship for Phari-Speaking Category: This scholarship is provided by the Department of Minority Affairs. Eligible students should apply through the relevant department portal.

Students are encouraged to check the eligibility criteria and apply accordingly to benefit from these scholarship opportunities.
